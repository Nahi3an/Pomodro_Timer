What is Pomodoro Technique?

Pomodro is a Time Management Technique created by Francesco Cirillo for a more productive way to manage your work and study. For more detailed info visit Pomodro Technique

How to use Pomodoro Technique?

1. Devide your work session into small segments of 20-25 minutes.
2. Take a small break of 5 minutes after completing each session.
3. Take a long break of 10 to 20 minutes after completing a full circle.

How to use this App?
